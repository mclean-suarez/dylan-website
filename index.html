Adjectives are guesses; examples are patterns
“Professional but warm.” “Punchy.” “On-brand.” These are adjectives — a fuzzy target Claude has to guess at. Give it a pattern instead: show two or three outputs you’d have been happy with, and tell it to match them.
Ask AIon this lesson
What does “few-shot” mean here?
A “shot” is one worked example you hand Claude — so few-shot just means giving it a few examples to copy instead of a description.
