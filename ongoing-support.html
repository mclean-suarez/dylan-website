Frequently asked questions

What is Ongoing Support?
It's an optional layer on top of the course that keeps your team sharp as AI keeps moving — webinars, tool masterclasses, business-application podcasts, a newsletter, a weekly support hour and a peer network.
Is Ongoing Support included in the program?
No. It's an optional add-on after the program, not a core inclusion. The program itself includes lifetime academy access.
What's actually included?
AI trends webinars, tool masterclasses (recorded and kept), business-application podcasts, a regular newsletter, a weekly support hour and access to a peer network.
Why would my team need it?
AI changes fast. Ongoing Support keeps your people current after the program so the skills they built keep pace with new tools and capabilities.
