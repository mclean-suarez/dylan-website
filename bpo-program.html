Staged progression

From novice to advanced AI user.

Three foundational months grounded in each person’s real work — then the program continues, tailored to their specific role.

Month 1

Orientation & possibilities

Set up tools, learn safe AI usage, and identify where AI supports each participant’s specific responsibilities.

Month 2

Prompt mastery

Build repeatable prompting methods, create templates for frequent tasks, and reduce rework through clearer instructions.

Month 3

Applying AI to your workflow

Convert learning into practical, repeatable workflows — integrating AI into real process steps and demonstrating it in-role.

Month 4 & beyond

Role-specific training

Participants are grouped by role — admin, marketing, finance, EA/VA and more — and training keeps evolving around the tasks, tools and workflows each person actually uses.

Frequently asked questions

Who is the BPO AI Program for?
It's built specifically for BPO providers and outsourced teams who want to turn their people into confident, productive AI users. It's a separate product from our main SME program.
How is the program structured?
It runs on a monthly cycle across three months, taking each person from novice to advanced AI user with structured, role-relevant training.
What does each month cover?
The cycle moves through orientation and possibilities, prompt mastery, applying AI to your actual workflow, and role-specific training — so the skills map to the work your people already do.
How is this different from the standard program?
The BPO AI Program is tailored to outsourced and offshore teams and the way they work, rather than the in-house SME staff the main AI Training & Enablement program is built for.
What happens after month three?
There's support to keep the momentum going after the program, so your team keeps building on what they've learnt rather than losing it.
