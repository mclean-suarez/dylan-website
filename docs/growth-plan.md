# Ad On AI — SEO + AEO Growth Plan

## North star
Turn organic search + AI-answer visibility into book-a-call enquiries. Strategy: **win informational/AEO query clusters with content now** (proven — the AI-agent article ranks with zero backlinks), **earn a small number of authoritative links + entity signals**, and **convert readers** with tools and CTAs.

## Baseline (July 2026)
- Technical SEO: complete (deep schema, sitemap, robots, llms.txt, clean URLs, fast static content pages, NAP, GBP linked as sameAs).
- Content: 9 resources articles + glossary. The AI-agent article is the #1 impression driver.
- GSC: ~84 impressions, 11 clicks, 13.1% CTR, avg position ~33.8. Queries dominated by the "AI agent" cluster + branded ("ad on", "adon ai").
- Off-page: near-zero backlinks; new/low-authority domain.

## Guiding priorities
1. **Content velocity is the biggest lever we control** — it ranks without links, and compounds.
2. **A few authoritative links beat many weak ones** — Anthropic/partner listings + AOG sites.
3. **The research report bridges on-page and off-page** — content that earns links.
4. Everything ships to `staging`; **merge to `main`** to go live.
