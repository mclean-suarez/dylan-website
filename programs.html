Frequently asked questions

How long is the AI Training & Enablement program?
It runs over three months and is self-paced, at around two hours per week. You can go faster or slower, but three months gives the knowledge time to sink in and be applied to real work.
Who is the program designed for?
Non-technical staff at Australian small-to-medium businesses — roles like finance, operations, marketing, admin and customer service. No technical background is required.
What will each staff member be able to do by the end?
Every learner finishes with 20+ working AI builds tied to their own job — reusable prompt packs, custom skills, automations, end-to-end workflows and deployed AI agents — plus a record of the hours reclaimed.
Is this just a video course?
No. It's a managed program, not a video library. Every module includes a test or practical project; learners can't progress without completing it, and managers are notified if someone stalls.
Does it only work with Claude?
No. The skills are tool-agnostic by design and transfer to ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini and whatever comes next. Claude is used as the worked example.
What happens after the three months?
Learners keep lifetime academy access, and optional ongoing support — webinars, masterclasses, podcasts and a newsletter — is available to keep skills current as AI moves.
