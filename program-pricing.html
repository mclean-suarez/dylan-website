FAQs

The questions we hear most.

Can I cancel?
Yes, any time.
How do I know they’re actually doing it?
Every module includes a test or practical project. They can’t progress without completing it, and you’re notified if they stall.
What if they miss a session?
The program is self-paced — there are no enforced deadlines. All sessions are recorded and stay in the academy for life. Each module requires submitted evidence to progress, so they move when they’re ready.
How fast will I see results?
Most people start using AI in their daily work from month one — you’ll notice the difference before you read a report.
Can new staff join mid-program?
Yes. New intake starts every month at level 1.
What if we don’t use Claude?
The skills are tool-agnostic by design — they transfer to ChatGPT, Copilot, Gemini and whatever comes next.
Can I add more staff later?
Yes. Monthly intake is limited so each person gets proper support — it’s best to plan ahead, as some months have a waiting list.
Is there support after the program?
Yes — lifetime academy access plus ongoing access to webinars, podcasts, masterclasses & newsletters, designed to keep your team current as AI moves.
